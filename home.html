Fun fact:

Where did Dunder Mifflin come from you ask? It started when two men named Robert Dunder, and Robert Mifflin met at a Dartmouth college visit. Dunder Mifflin was created originally to sell brackets of use of construction in New York City. Dunder Mifflin is now a small paper company branching out of New York City with many small branching in the Northern Hemisphere including our very own, Scranton, Pennsylvania.
